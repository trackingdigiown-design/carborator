About Us

Our Story

Pacoima's Carburetor Specialists

Valley Carburetor Co. was built on a simple promise: give every customer — whether a first-timer or a seasoned fleet manager — the best carburetor service in Los Angeles, at a fair price, done fast.

Located in the heart of Pacoima on San Fernando Road, we've grown into one of the most trusted names in carburetor wholesale, retail, and rebuilding in the greater LA area. Our shop handles everything from classic American muscle cars to modern foreign imports, heavy industrial equipment, and warehouse forklifts.

We specialize in speed without cutting corners. Our signature "Rebuilt in 3 Hours" service means you won't be waiting days for your carburetor. Walk in, drop it off, and in most cases you'll have it back the same afternoon.

Our wholesale program serves auto dealers, repair shops, and fleet operators across California. Bulk orders, competitive pricing, and reliable stock — because your business can't afford downtime.

"At Valley Carburetor Co., we treat every carburetor like it's going into our own vehicle. That's the standard we've always held — and always will."

— Luis & Angel, Valley Carburetor Co.
